Shehu is a web developer located in New Haven, CT. He graduated from Central Connecticut State University with a degree in Computer Science. Shehu started his career in web development in 2018. He makes most of his websites from scratch, but he has experience creating websites using templates as well.

Shehu worked at CueScript as a JavaScript Developer for about two years. Now he would like to focus his talents on creating custom websites as a freelancer. If you click on the project's link, you can see all the websites Shehu has created and is currently working on. The link to his GitHub is shown at the top of the project's page.

Shehu would like to make websites for small businesses to help grow their customer base. He can make 3 page static websites in as little as two weeks for a small fee of $1,000 - $1,500. Shehu has some experience making responsive websites. He would like to work remotely so that he can travel to different parts of the world making websites for people who are need of websites, but may not know how to make them or are too busy to make one.
